The DART Team

FOUNDER

Chris McMurtry is a classical composer, rock guitarist, and tech geek. He learned the hard way that it was much easier to publish rock and other music genres to online stores than it was to publish classical music. So he decided to do something about it. He enlisted the help of two of the most talented people he knows who also happen to be his best friends. Together they are changing the world of classical music.

DEVELOPER

David Stevenson is a programmer, musician and writer. His band, Manic Bloom, has had their music featured everywhere from MTV’s Real World to Oxygen’s Bad Girl’s Club without the help of a label or manager. When not playing music or typing feverishly, he’s tending to his chickens and honey bees, and working to get off the grid in general. (Well - except for the internet. He’ll always need the internet.)

MARKETER

Richard Jacobson is an animator, graphic designer, published writer and presenter. Currently he creates animations for his popular animated video and cartoon blog as well as animation tutorials for his other blog. Plus he creates and self-publishes his own interactive iPad books. And he spearheads all the content creation and social media for DART. That means he spends a lot of time playing on his Mac, iPad and iPhone.
