The DART Team

FOUNDER

Chris McMurtry is a classical composer, rock guitarist, and tech geek. He learned the hard way that it was much easier to publish rock and other music genres to online stores than it was to publish classical music. So he decided to do something about it. He enlisted the help of two of the most talented people he knows who also happen to be his best friends. Together they are changing the world of classical music.
